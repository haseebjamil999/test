About GreenBacks Trading SDN BHD

GreenBacks Trading SDN BHD is run by an experienced director who oversees the existing company model and acts as a mediator between customers and suppliers. The primary concentration is on merchanting trade, which involves sourcing things from one country and shipping them to destinations outside Malaysia.

GreenBacks Trading serves as a crucial link in the supply chain, facilitating smooth transactions and building enduring relationships with clients and partners. Our Merchanting Trade knowledge allows us to easily source and distribute products across international borders.

Our Mission

To bridge global markets by providing exceptional merchanting trade services that connect suppliers and customers worldwide with reliability, efficiency, and trust.
